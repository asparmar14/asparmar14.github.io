Customer Retention Strategy

→ Diagnosed churn drivers in weeks 4–8 · Targeted +15% retention improvement

Problem
  • ▸Month-3 retention at ~54% — 11pts below industry benchmark (~65%)
  • ▸Churn clustering in weeks 4–8, before habit formation
Key Metrics
Week-1 / Week-4 / Month-3 Retention Skip Rate Churn by Channel 90-day Reactivation
Insights
  • ▸2+ consecutive skips → 3× higher churn probability
  • ▸Early customisers retained at 71% vs 46% — a 25-point gap
  • ▸Paid social cohorts churned ~40% faster than organic
Recommendations
  • ①Trigger skip intervention at first skip — recipe swap prompt
  • ②"Build your box" onboarding to drive early customisation
  • ③Shift budget to channels with better 90-day retention, not just low CAC
📈 Estimated Impact

Month-3 retention: 54% → 67% · Potential +15% improvement

Problem
